Sunni and Shia Beliefs
⟩
Sunni
⟩
When Muhammed died, the majority of Muslims thought that only the Qur'an and the Sunnah (Muhammad's teachings) had authority
They elected Abu Bakr (Muhammad's closest friend) to be their leader (Caliph), to act on behalf of Allah and Muhammad to ensure people followed Allah's laws.
Caliphs did not make the laws, they enforced them once the community had taken the views from scholars, the Qur'an and Sunnahs
Sunni means followers of the Sunnah. They form the majority of the Muslim community

Prophethood
⟩
Adam
⟩
Considered to be the father of the human race and the first prophet
Adam is important to Muslims because God gave him knowledge and understanding 
God taught him how to live a good life in obedience to God, and Adam passed on his knowledge to the rest of the human race through his descendants
Ibrahim
⟩
He refused to worship idols and preached that there is only one God
He rebuilt the Ka'aba after it was destroyed in the great flood 
He was willing to sacrifice his son to God.
He is a good role model for Muslims because he always had faith in God and showed obedience to God at a time when many people worshipped a variety of gods and idols (statues)
