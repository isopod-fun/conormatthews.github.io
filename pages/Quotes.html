Chapters 6-10
⟩
"A great curiosity came at once to the trustee to disregard the prohibition, and dive at once to the bottom of these mysteries; but professional honour and faith to his good friend were stringent obligations; and the packet slept in the inmost corner of his private safe." Narrator talking about the letter from Lanyon that says "Not to be opened till the death or disappearance of Dr. Henry Jekyll"
⟩
Theme of loyalty and honour
⟩
He doesn't go against the wishes of a friend even in the most compelling circumstances. 
Draws out and intensifies the mystery.
"'Oh God' I screamed, and 'Oh God!' again and again; for there before my eyes - pale and shaken, and half fainting, and groping before him with his hands, like a man restored from death - there stood Henry Jekyll!" Lanyon describing transformation from Hyde to Jekyll
⟩
First description of a character witnessing the transformation in the novel.
The repetition of "'Oh God!'" shows that even though Lanyon is a man of science he is so shocked, he can do nothing but call for God. Shows the scale of the event.
"All thinks therefore seemed to point to this: that I was slowly losing hold of my original and better self, and slowly becoming incorporated with my second and worse." Jekyll describing when he woke up as Hyde without drinking the potion.
⟩
Shows the fatal flaw in Jekyll's experiment.
⟩
Whilst the evil part of Jekyll's personality is physically smaller, it has now become more powerful.
The drug he had taken to allow him freedom and pleasure has now become a necessity.
⟩
He needs it to change back from Hyde to Jekyll rather than the other way around.
⟩
His need is desperate.
"Much of his past was unearthed, indeed, and all disreputable: tales came out of the man's cruelty, at once so callous and violent, of his vile life, of his strange associates, of the hatred that seemed to have surrounded his career; but of his present whereabouts, not a whisper" Narrator talking about the unusual disappearance of Hyde.
⟩
His disappearance coincides with the sudden reappearance of Jekyll who is in good health.
The disappearance is unusual because of his powerful presence.
